Professional & legal framework

Scope of our role

  • We provide coordination and communication between patients, doctors, and the pharmacy.
  • We help clarify which medications are available and legal in Indonesia.
  • We do not replace the clinical judgement of doctors or pharmacists.

Key principles

  • Prescription-only medicines require a valid prescription from a licensed doctor.
  • No controlled substances, narcotics, or prohibited drugs are supplied.
  • All dispensing and record-keeping are performed by the pharmacy partner.
