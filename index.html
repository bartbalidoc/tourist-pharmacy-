How Prescriptions Work

  • All prescription-only medications require a valid prescription from a licensed doctor.
  • Prescriptions may be issued by Indonesian doctors or, where legally permitted, by doctors from other jurisdictions.
  • PharmaCare checks each prescription for completeness, safety, and appropriateness.
  • We do not supply controlled substances or medications prohibited by Indonesian law.
  • Medications are dispensed from PharmaCare’s licensed premises and are not exported outside Indonesia.

How to Order Right Now

Until our online ordering system launches, ordering is handled manually:

  1. Contact Us
    Send us a message via WhatsApp at +62 895-1802-3363 or email us at info@balidoc.com and tell us what medication you need and where you are staying.
  2. Prescription & Availability Check
    If the medication requires a prescription, we will ask you for a valid prescription from a licensed doctor. PharmaCare will confirm availability and whether the medication can be dispensed legally.
  3. Dispensing & Pickup / Local Delivery
    Your medication is prepared and dispensed by PharmaCare at Jl. Sidakarya No.92, Sidakarya, Denpasar Selatan, Kota Denpasar, Bali 80224. Pickup or local delivery may be arranged according to pharmacy policy.

All orders are currently handled manually to keep the process safe and fully compliant. Online payment and automated ordering will be added later.

Patient Safety & Compliance

  • Only medications from approved, legal suppliers are dispensed.
  • All dispensing is done by licensed pharmacists at PharmaCare.
  • We comply with Indonesian regulations on pharmacy practice, prescriptions, and data protection.
  • We do not promote or sell medications in ways that conflict with regulations of Indonesia or patients’ home countries.
